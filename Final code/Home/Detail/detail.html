Chiang Kai-Shek Memorial Hall ( 中正紀念堂 )

The National Chiang Kai-shek Memorial Hall (Chinese: 國立中正紀念堂; Pe̍h-ōe-jī: Kok-li̍p Tiong-chèng-kí-liām-tn̂g) is a national monument, landmark and tourist attraction erected in memory of Generalissimo Chiang Kai-shek, former President of the Republic of China.
